Summer 2023

I received a SURA undergraduate research award for the summer of 2023. This allowed me to become an intern at MILA - Quebec AI Institute, where I joined Prof. Siamak Ravanbakhsh's research group.

At MILA, I am working on improving current machine learning models for solving partial differential equations. In particular, I am trying to improve models called physics-informed DeepONets.

MATH 410 - Major's Research Project

During the winter 2023 semester, I undertook a research project under the supervision of Dr. Jeremy Macdonald and Prof. Marcin Sabok.

In this project, I studied a fundamental problem lying at the intersection of combinatorial group theory and computability theory: the word problem for groups. In particular, I studied the connections between group theoretic properties of a group, and language theoretic properties of its word problem.
